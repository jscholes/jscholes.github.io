Unexpected Focus Movement

This page includes a check box, followed by a text field.

Start by double tapping on the text field, to show the on-screen keyboard. Then, double tap the check box to toggle its value.

Notice that when interaction occurs with the check box, the on-screen keyboard disappears and VoiceOver focus confusingly returns to the text field. However, document.activeElement (i.e. the system focus) is undefined or on the body.
